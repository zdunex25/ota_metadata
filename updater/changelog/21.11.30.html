CHANGELOG 21.11.30


System
New - Android 12 update (Mi 10, Mi 10 Pro, Redmi K30 Pro/POCO F2 Pro, Redmi K30S Ultra/Mi 10T)

Security
Fix - "Optimized charging" settings were missing on the supported devices

Gallery
Optimization - Optimized editing for screenshots

For Redmi K30 Pro/POCO F2 Pro and Redmi K30S Ultra/Mi 10T users: There's a chance (~50%) that your device might be bricked after installing this update. Please back up your data before upgrading to Android 12. You've been warned, please flash with your own risk, Xiaomi.EU is not responsible for any damages done to your device(s).
That message doesn't concern Mi 10/Pro users.

Tip for Android 12 devices: Because TWRP doesn't work with Android 12, the update needs to be flashed in Fastboot mode. Download the ZIP package, unpack it and run update script (without formatting data if you're moving from Android 11 xiaomi.eu ROM or with format data if you're flashing it first time).

Known Android 12 bugs:
* If you're from Thailand, Malaysia, or Vietnam, please change MIUI Region to China to temporarily solve Wi-Fi disconnecting occasionally issues.
* The MIUI team received reports from the Chinese users regarding unknown cause of app removal, however, after inspection, the app data will be kept. Users may redownload the specific app if found missing for the time being, the team is working on it.

This changelog doesn't mention all changes made by Xiaomi.eu or Xiaomi.
