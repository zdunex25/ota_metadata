CHANGELOG OS1.0.24.7.28.DEV


Today's Weekly ROM release marks the end of Weekly history. Xiaomi stated that the company will no longer distribute Weekly ROMs.

We truly appreciate your testing, cooperation, and sharing of your thoughts and observations regarding the weekly ROMs. We hope that this marks a new beginning rather than the end!

As far as we know, it is likely that the upcoming, new "beta" branch will be built upon stable releases. Switch to these releases as soon as you can. We don't know at this point what it will look like; we'll keep you informed as we find out.

Thank you!

This changelog doesn't mention all changes made by Xiaomi.eu or Xiaomi.
