Because TWRP doesn't work with Android 12, the update needs to be flashed in Fastboot mode. Download the ZIP package, unpack it and run update script (without formatting data if you're moving from Android 11 xiaomi.eu ROM or with format data if you're flashing it first time).

Warning: Camera doesn't work properly on Mi 10T Pro. We don't recommend upgrading to this version if you own Mi 10T Pro. Redmi K30S Ultra/Mi 10T camera works fine.
Due to the Android 12 upgrade, Xiaomi MIX 4 weekly ROM releases are temporarily suspended starting from 25 October.
Starting from 27 November there will be no Beta ROMs for following devices: Mi 9, Mi 9 SE, Mi 9T/Redmi K20, Mi 9T Pro/Redmi K20 Pro, Mi 9 Lite/Mi CC9, Mi 9 Pro 5G, Redmi Note 8.

Known Android 12 issues:
* Wi-Fi issues (randomly disconnect and won't connect back until Wi-Fi is turned off and on again). If you face it in Europe, change region to United Kingdom. If you face it in Asia, change region to China [Known MIUI China issue]
* Privacy mark on the status bar cannot be turned off [Known MIUI China issue]
* Sometimes unlocking device can lead to a blackscreen, turn screen on/off and unlock again will fix it, and sometimes lead to System UI crash [Known MIUI China issue]
* Clear recent apps may sometimes lead to soft reboot [Reported by CN users, unaddressed by the development team (UI)]

This changelog doesn't mention all changes made by Xiaomi.eu or Xiaomi.
