CHANGELOG 21.11.10


System
Optimization - Updated Android Security Patch to November 2021
Fix - Sometimes "Smart toolbox" bar position was displayed incorrectly
Fix - Icons weren't changed while applying a theme in some cases

Control center
Fix - Sometimes notifications were flashing when swiping left and right to switch between Notification shade and Control center

Settings
Fix - Location settings stopped working on some devices
Fix - Couldn't adjust the font size occassionally after adjusting the size several times in a short period of time

Tip for Android 12 devices: Because TWRP doesn't work with Android 12, the update needs to be flashed in Fastboot mode. Download the ZIP package, unpack it and run update script (without formatting data if you're moving from Android 11 xiaomi.eu ROM or with format data if you're flashing it first time).

Starting from 8 November, Beta ROMs for Mi 10, Mi 10 Pro, Mi 10 Ultra, Redmi K30 Pro/POCO F2 Pro, Redmi K30S Ultra/Mi 10T/Pro, Xiaomi Civi are temporarily suspended due to Android 12 tests.

Starting from 27 November there will be no Beta ROMs for following devices: Mi 9, Mi 9 SE, Mi 9T/Redmi K20, Mi 9T Pro/Redmi K20 Pro, Mi 9 Lite/Mi CC9, Mi 9 Pro 5G, Redmi Note 8.

Known Android 12 issues:
* If you're from Thailand, Malaysia, or Vietnam, please change MIUI Region to China to temporarily solve Wi-Fi disconnecting occasionally issues.
